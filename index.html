Poplar Variety W109

W109 is one of the most popular and high-performing poplar clones in India. It is known for its fast growth, straight stem, and high timber yield, making it a farmer‚Äôs favorite.

Poplar Variety W110

W110 is another premium clone with a reputation for uniform growth and good disease tolerance. It balances fast maturity with quality timber production.

Poplar Variety W111

W111 is a newer poplar variety, preferred by some farmers for its quick initial growth and good adaptability in irrigated conditions.

Poplar Variety W112

W112 is also a fast-growing clone but with limited trial data compared to W109/W110. It is used by farmers who experiment with new varieties for potentially higher returns.
